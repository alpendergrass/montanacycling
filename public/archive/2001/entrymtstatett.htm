Championships

Categories:                 USCF Junior, Master, Pro 1,2,3.  4,5.

                                    PLUS Citizen class!  OPEN to ALL!

 

Course:                       20 and 40km.  Out and Back.

                                    Flat and Fast (see edge, any edge of paper for course profile).

 

The race will be held on the old state TT course in Belgrade, MT. 

A Montana Classic.

 

Awards:                      Full-on USCF State Championship Medals!

AND the TT is on the first day of a two-day omnium where there is $1000 in prize money plus merchandise!

 

TT Medals will be given after the TT.  Omnium cash and prizes will be given out after Sunday’s Road Race.

 

Entry Fee:                  TT only -- $20

                                    TT and RR (Omnium) -- $35

                                    Citizen TT -- $10

 

When:                         June 30, 2001

All sign in: 8:30 to 9:10

Citizen Class 9:30am. 

USCF 10am.

Registration:

Only citizen racers may register the day of the race.  Day of race registration ends at 9am.
