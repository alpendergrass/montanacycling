The "Fairfield Road Race" was held May 12, 2001, in the shadow of the Rocky

Mountain Front. Weather was ideal for Bicycle Racing with light winds, blue

skies, and 2001's first day of warm temperatures. Forty-six racers turned

out to test their early season conditioning. No doubt about it, John Weyrich

of the 2,3 division and Rich West of the Masters division were flying and

ready for Ecology next weekend. Thanks to our sponsors, Knicker Biker,

Bighorn Wilderness, Central Bike, and Great Harvest Bread, for making the

race possible.

Thanks especially to my wife, Kristen, who stepped in for our Chief Official

when he had to cancel. Racers and volunteers alike made this an increasingly

enjoyable race. Thanks to ALL
