JEDZILLA's

Mountain Bike Race

September 15 and 16, 2001

Missoula, Montana

What

The Jedzilla Mountain Bike Race is a challenging cross country and downhill mountain bike race for beginners and experts alike.

Where

Race begins and ends at the equestrian parking lot at the Blue Mountain Rec Area located 1 mile North of Highway 93 on Blue Mountain Road. Take Highway 93 South of Missoula 2 miles, then turn right on Blue Mountain Road and the start/finish area will be well marked.

When

Saturday September 15th, 2001 DOWNHILL

Registration 3:00 pm Racing Starts 4:30 pm

Sunday September 16th, 2001 CROSS COUNTRY

Registration 8:00 am Racing Starts 9:30 am

Registration at the Blue Mountain Equestrian Parking Lot. See above for directions.

 

Entry Fee:

$ 10 Downhill

$15 Cross Country

$ 20 for both events

 

Awards

Awards will be held at the Start Finish Line as soon as possible after the race

Awards will be given to the top male and female finishers in all Norba Catagories.

There are Expert, Sport and Beginner classes for each age division.
