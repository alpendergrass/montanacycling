CROSS SERIES RANKINGS CALCULATIONS:

Riders are given one point for finishing each race plus one point for each person they beat in their catagory including DNFs. In addition, top five in each catagory are given the following bonus points- 8,5,3,2,1. State Championship race awards extra bonus points of 4,3,2,1 to top five in addition to other points. Top five scores for each rider are scored. Ties broken by head to head analysis.
