Attached are the results of the Missoula Training Series #4, Butler

Creek Circuit Race #2, One hour + 1 lap, 22 entrants.

Special thanks to our volunteers Kathleen Jones and Charlie Beaton. I'd

also like to thank Big Sky Cyclery for the use of their shop for

registration and everyone for participating.

This was our third year for this series and the participation has grown

every year with over 125 racers this year. The goal of these races is

to promote affordable convenient races for everyone from beginner to

expert. Thanks to everyone for making this a huge success. Hope to see

everyone next spring!

 

Fred
