Third Annual Western Montana Spring Training Series

Missoula Montana

* Saturday Mar.17 - Clinton Road Race 1 - 28 miles.

* Saturday Mar. 24 - Butler Creek Circuit Race 1 - 1 Hour

* Saturday Mar. 31 - Clinton Road Race II - 43 Miles.

* Saturday April 21 - Butler Creek Circuit Race II - 1 Hour

 

Start time for all races is 12:00 noon.

Registration for all races will be at the Big Sky Cyclery. (1110 South

Ave. West across from Fairgrounds) from 10:30 - 11:00.

Course Descriptions:

* Butler Creek Circuit - Hilly 1.7 mile circuit. Start is at

intersection of Expressway Ave. and Butler Creek Rd.

* Clinton Road Race - Mostly flat 8 mile circuit. Start is in

Clinton at intersection of frontage road and I-90 overpass. Finish

is @ three miles past the start on south frontage road.

Entry fee: $5.00 per race.

Categories are: A - USCF 1 - 4. * B - Women, USCF 5, Citizens and Juniors. Masters race their USCF category.

Races will be handicapped, with category B riders given a head start. All racers finish together and on the same lap. Lapped riders are encouraged to continue, but no contesting final main field sprint. Free lap for mechanical problems except for the Clinton RR. If weather is totally miserable (< 45 degrees with rain or snow - race will be postponed to Sunday).

Open to all cyclists, regardless of ability level. The purpose of this series is to gain experience and fitness with low key, fun races. Cyclists are encouraged to ride to the races from registration and continue after with a training ride through the lovely Montana countryside.

Races are sponsored by Five Valley Velo USCF Permit Pending.
