EVENT DESCRIPTION

The Blackfoot River Brewing Company Criterium course is located in the Capitol Complex in Helena, Montana. The 0.7-mile almost flat course is set near the State Capitol Building among shady tree-lined roads of the Capitol State Park, and will provide a fast and fun course for all racers. The race will also serve as the Montana Criterium Championships. Registration is located near the course at the rear of the Capitol Building on Lockey Avenue. Race day registration only. There will also be two free fun races for children 10 and under.

All USCF rules and Junior gear restrictions will be enforced. Helmets are mandatory for all racers.
