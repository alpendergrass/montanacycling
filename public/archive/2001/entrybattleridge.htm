New Course!

This years’ edition of the Battle Ridge Road Race will start at the beautiful Bohart Ranch at mile 16 on Bridger Canyon Road just ½ mile from the turnoff to the Bridger Bowl ski area. The course will include lots of climbing at the beginning and end of the race, a flat to rolling middle section and an uphill finish. Not to mention drop-dead gorgeous Montana scenery.

· Registration at Bohart Ranch starting at 8:00 a.m. Saturday July 1st.

  • Entry Fee: $20 Race Day Registration Only! $35 combined entry fee for Battle Ridge R.R. and State Time Trial Omnium. Register for both events on Saturday June 30th at the State Time Trial in Belgrade.
  • 10:00 race start: Cat. 1,2,3 Men. Course distance 70 mile from Bohart to Clyde Park and back.
  • 10:10 race start: All other U.S.C.F. categories. Course distance 50 miles from Bohart to Junction of highways 86 and 89 and back.
  • Categories may be combined if fields are small at the discretion of the promoter.
  • All U.S.C.F. rules apply. Licenses required at registration. Race day (Cat. 5) and annual licenses available at registration.
  • Awards ceremony to be held at Bohart Ranch directly following the finish of the race.
  • All information in this bulletin subject to change. Please verify start time and other event issues at registration.

Directions to Bohart Ranch:

From I-90 take any exit to Bozeman. Follow directions to Main Street. On Main and Rouse turn North on Rouse and follow signs to Bridger Bowl. Bohart Ranch is ½ a mile further north on Bridger Canyon road from the turnoff to Bridger Bowl.
