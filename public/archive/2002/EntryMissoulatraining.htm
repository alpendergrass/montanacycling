Team Stampede / Five Valley Velo presents:

Fourth Annual Western Montana Spring Training Series

Missoula Montana

 

* Saturday March 09 - Clinton Road Race I - 35 miles.

* Saturday March 16 - Butler Creek Circuit Race I - 1 Hour + 1 lap.

* Saturday March 23 - Clinton Road Race II - 43 Miles.

Start time for all races is 12:00 noon.

Registration for all races will be at the Missoula Bike Works (521 South Higgins Ave). 10:30 - 11.

Course Descriptions:

* Butler Creek Circuit - Hilly 1.7 mile circuit. Start is at intersection of Expressway Ave. and Butler Creek Rd.

* Clinton Road Race - Mostly flat 8 mile circuit. Start is in Clinton at intersection of frontage road and I-90 overpass. Finish is @ three miles past the start on south frontage road.

Entry fee: $5.00 per race.

Categories are:

* A - Men USCF 1 - 4.

* B - Women, USCF 5, Citizens and Juniors.

Masters race their USCF category.

This is a USCF Sanctioned Event.
