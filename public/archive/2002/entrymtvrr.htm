Montana Velo Road Race is on for

Date: Sunday, April 14th 2002

Time: Racing begins at 11:00 sharp

Entry Fee: $17.00

USAC Permit # 2002-000554

 

Distances: Cat. 1,2,3 - 60 miles

Cat. 4,5, Masters, Jrs, Women 40 miles

Course Description: A flat 20 mile circuit winding its way through the scenic

Helena Valley. There is a few mile section of dirt road with a short hill and

another brief section of rough pavement. The rest is smooth sailing.

 

Registration will be from 9:30-10:30 a.m. at Rossiter Elementary, 1497 E.

Sierra Rd.

Use the north doors for registration and bathrooms. There will be a church

service in progress in the gym, so please be respectful.

 

To get there, get yourself to Helena and get on Montana Ave., go North a mile

or two from the intersection of Montana and Custer where there is a

McDonalds, a Shopko and a Hastings. Go straight(North) to Sierra Rd., hang a

right (East) and you're at Rossiter Elementary School (just before the I-15

overpass).

 

The start and the finish will be right there in that area. Hope to see you

there!
