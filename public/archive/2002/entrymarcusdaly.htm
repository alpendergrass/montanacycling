Marcus Daly Time Trial and Criterium

(Montana State Championships)

Hamilton, Montana

August Third and Fourth 2002

$ 300 prize purse

Time Trial Registration- Saturday 2 pm to 4 pm at Valley Bike and Ski in Hamilton on Highway 93 (219 S. First) just South of Main Street.

Time Trial Racing- First rider off at 5pm Saturday. Riders will go by division in the following order: (Masters 45+, Masters 25-44, Juniors, Cat 4,5 Men, Women 1,2,3,4, Cat 1,2,3 Men)

Time Trial Course- 40k (24.8 miles) located just Southeast of Hamilton on Sleeping Child Road. Course is slight uphill out, fast downhill back.

Criterium Registration-Saturday 2pm to 4 pm at Valley Bike and Ski and 7 am to 8:30 am on Sunday morning at the Start Finish line on Main Street.

Criterium Racing- Sunday-9:00 am Masters and Juniors (40 minutes); 10:50- Cat 4,5 men (40 minutes); 11:40 Women 1,2,3,4 (40 minutes); Men 1,2,3 (50 minutes)

Criterium Course-- Downtown Hamilton, L shaped course with 5 right turns and one left turn; fast and flat

Medals and Cash to top three in each race.

Entry Fee is $ 16 for both Crit and TT, $ 12 for either Crit or TT

No pre-registration, register at race.

Race Director- Randy Lavall 406.363.4428
