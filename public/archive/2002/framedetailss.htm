Win Two Gift Certificates for Custom Frames!

Strong Frames believes in bike racing in Montana and they have donated two custom frames to the 2002 Montana racing series!

Racers will have their name entered into the drawing for the frame every time they race in a series event. In addition, racers will have their name added into the drawing an additional time every time they win their category in a series event.

At the end of the year we will hold a random drawing and award one frame to someone from the Mountain Bike Series and one frame to someone from the Road Racing Series. Each winner will receive a certificate for a standard custom designed and painted frame from Carl Strong and the rest of the folks in Bozeman.

In addition special awards will be given to the series leaders at the end of the year.

Many Thanks To All of the Race Directors and To Strong Frames!
