RACE DESCRIPTION:

The road race will be held on the frontage (Hwy 91) south of Butte between Feely and Melrose.  The Feely-Melrose race is part of the Montana Women’s series (Separate wheel cars will be provided).  Registration is from 10:00 AM – 11:00 A.M; the race will begin at 11:15 A.M.  Race Numbers on Right Side of Jersey.  All USCF Rules Apply

·         Race Day registration only

·         Fee is $20 – Juniors $10

·         Racing Starts at 11:15 AM

·         One-day license will be available for $5

·         Racers who do not possess a permanent MBRA race number must purchase a one-day number for $2 or a permanent MBRA race number for $15.

·        Promoters reserve the right to combine classes

·         Total prize purse $500

Directions to Feely (Registration)

From Butte, take I-90 west towards Missoula.  After 5 miles, you will see the I-15 South (Idaho Falls) exit, take it.  Travel south for 9 miles to the Feely exit (Exit 111); take this exit and turn west (right).  Registration will be near the beginning of the dirt road.
