Missoula Area Group Cycling Rides 2004

 

Glossary/Legend:

Weekend Group Ride:  Mixed bag of fitness levels but everyone sticks together.  Sometimes the pace picks up, but we will regroup.  Routes are predetermined and provide plenty of places to turn around.  Folks new to riding in packs are encouraged to attend.

 

Hell Ride:  A fast paced ride.  No waiting, all-out-hammerfest!

 

Turah TT:  A 10 mile time trial (race against the clock)  Departs from Turah cutoff

 

Newbie ride:  Group ride for riders new to riding in groups and is usually easy to moderate pace.  Departs from Missoula Bicycle Works.

 

D.E.A.T.H. Ride:  Corbin’s sick idea.  Drop Everyone Ascending The Hills:  Climb all hills around Missoula with points being awarded to the top 3 finishers.  3pts for 1st to top, 2 for 2nd, and 1 for 3rd.  Ice Cream and bragging rights to the winner.
