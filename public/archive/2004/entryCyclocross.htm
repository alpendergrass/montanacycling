2004 Montana Cyclocross Schedule and Information

 

Greetings, Earthlings.

 

The cool thing about the new USACycling licensing scheme is that you can get a license that is good until the end of the year for $25. So do that. Or pay $5/race. All racers must have a current USACycling license.

 

The Big Sky Brewing Wednesday Cyclocross Series:

Entry fee:$5 per race or $20 for the series.

All races start at 5:45 pm. The tap room closes at 6:30 pm.

Races will be 50 minutes plus one lap.

All races are at the brewery on the western outskirts of Missoula, just off the airport exit on I-90.

This is a low-key series with lots of cool edible prizes and lots of beer at the finish line.

It is ideal for beginners, triathletes, old pros, former high school pole vaulters, and national champions alike. We have all of those here, so let’s get them out to the races. Mountain bikes will work just fine for this series if you don’t have a cross bike.

This is the real deal, with competition for the coveted Montana Series championship.

Entry fees vary from race to race, from around $15 to $25. 

Check montanacycling.org for specific information for each race.
