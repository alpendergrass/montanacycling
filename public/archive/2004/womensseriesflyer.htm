Montana Women’s Cycling Series

 

"The races to try if you have ever considered racing"

Also a competitive Race Series for Category 1,2 and 3 riders

Series Highlights

  • Four races spread throughout the state and throughout the cycling race season
  • Two points categories (1,2,3) and (4)
  • Separate women’s starts
  • Placement recognition for top 3 finishers in CAT 1,2,3 and CAT 4 at each race.
  • End of series award to overall winner and to the Team with greatest number of participants throughout the series
  • Camaraderie, Encouragement and Competition
