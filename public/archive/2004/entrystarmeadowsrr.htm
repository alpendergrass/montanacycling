Flathead Cycling presents the

Star Meadows Road Race

Saturday, May 8,2004

Race #4 of the MBRA Race Series

Race #2 of the Women on Wheels Series

Held under USCF permit # 2004-721

 

A road race in the scenic Salish Mountains northwest of Kalispell.

 

Distances: Men cat 1/2/3 - 70 miles W/ 2 intermediate sprint primes

 

                    Masters A, Men 4/5, Women 1/2 - 50 miles

 

                    Masters B, Women 3/4, Juniors - 30 miles (with plenty of climbing)

 

Registration starts @ 9am , cat 1/2/3 race @ 11am, cat 4/5 @ 11:05, Masters A, Wmn. 1/2 @ 11:10,

Masters B, Women 3/4, Juniors @ 11:15. Promoter reserves the right to combine categories depending on turnout.

 

Directions:  Hwy. 93 North through Kalispell. Left on Reserve Dr. (Home Depot on right), West on Reserve app. 4 miles. Right on Farm-to-Market Rd.(West Valley School will be in front of you). Drive north app. 16 miles to Star Meadows Rd. Left 1 mile to Round Meadows XC ski area.

 

Entry fee is $20. One Day licenses and MBRA memberships will be available.
