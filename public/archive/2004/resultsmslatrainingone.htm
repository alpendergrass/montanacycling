Attached are the results for the first training race, but with some caveats.

We're also testing video camera results and as a result were not able to pick

all the numbers.  I also don't have the start list (I'm out of town for the

week), so just numbers will have to suffice for now.

 

We had approximately 65 starters for the handicap style race.  The "B" field

was given a five minute head start and still finished well clear of the "A"

field.  Same format next week but add an additional lap (eight miles) for a

total of 43 miles.

 

Congratulations to our Winners:

Jeff Cincoski - University of Montana Cycling

Jennifer Nerison - University of Montana Cycling

 

Thanks to our volunteers, the race couldn't have happened without their help.

Thanks also to The Big Dipper for the ice cream certificates.
