Directions to Thompson Park (Registration)

Take the Harrison exit (Exit 127A) south on Harrison Ave for 2.9 miles.  At the intersection of State Route 2 and Harrison Ave., turn southeast for approximately 4-miles (1-mile past the intersection of Continental Drive and State Route 2).  Signs will mark the turn-off to Thompson Park.
