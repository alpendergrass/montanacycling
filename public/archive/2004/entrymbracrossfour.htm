MBRA Cyclocross Series Race Number Four

Saturday October the 30th

Location: Big Sky Brewery, just west of Missoula.

(To get there take Airport Road exit off I-90, head south, and take the first right onto Harrier Way. The brewery is the large building with strange grain storage devices on the outside and steam pouring from vents on the roof. You might also notice a smell of malting barley.)

Start time: 12:00 noon.

Registration will begin at 11:00 at the brewery.

Entry fee: $10

Please bring your MBRA numbers if you have them. This is an MBRA series, so MBRA number requirements apply. We will have MBRA numbers at the race.

WARNING:             This is a new venue that we tried-out for the first time this year and it has proven to be a rough and bumpy course. If you are concerned about course roughness, you may want to consider bringing a mountain bike and putting it in the pit and switching from cross bike to mountain bike as needed. Mountain bikes are OK in cross, just remove bar ends if you have them.
