Sunday, November 14, 2004
Helena High School
Helena MT
USCF Permit #:2004-1975

Under unseasonably warm skies and little wind, Northern Rockies Jason Mohr rode a strong and complete race to win the Montana Men's Elite State Championship. Dueling with Montana Velo's Shane Mundt for the entire race on the very fast but technical Helena High circuit , Mohr capitalized on a small last lap mistake by Mundt to take the title.

Kathleen Jones (Five Valley Velo) rode uncontested to take the women's title and John Weyrhich (Five Valley Velo) fought another on-the-rivet battle with Walt Chauner (Flathead Cycling) to take the Masters 45+ gold medal. 

Thanks go to all who showed up to spectate, to all Montana Velo teammates for helping out as well as Finish Referee Carl "The Champ" Anderberg, Chief Official Byron DeFord, Atticus Proctor, Sean Kiely, Jason Hobson, and the Helena School District No. 1 Maintenance Staff.
