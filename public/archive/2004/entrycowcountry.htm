Cow Country Classic

2004 Montana Road Race Championships

 

          Registration: 9am until 10:30 July 25th

 

          Where: Holter Lake Fishing Access. 3 miles NE of Wolf Creek, MT

 

          Schedule:     10:00 Mandatory Racers Meeting

                             10:15 Cat 1,2 &3 start

                             10:30 Cat 4 & 5 men

                             10:40 Master A’s & Women 1&2s

                             10:50 Master B’s & Juniors

                             11:00 Women 3 & 4s

 

Course: Men 1,2 &3s will head north along the recreation road to Ostermans, they will turn around and ride back through Wolf Creek onto Hiway 434 to Hiway 200 then in on hiway 287. The finish is located approximately 2 miles from the end of Hiway 287 before I15 and is a hill top finish.

 

Medals will be awarded in all categories (not age graded!)

Prize list is   $475.00

1,2&3          $180.00

4&5             $55.00

Master A     $55.00

Master B      $55.00

Women 3&4$55.00

Women 1&2 $75.00 (if applicable)

Jrs Medals only

 

Promoter reserves right to combine categories

 

Rules: USA cycling rules, neutral through Wolf Creek. Racers are responsible for obeying all traffic laws. Center line rule is in force throughout the entire race, including finish.

 

Entry Fee; $20.00 one day MBRA membership $2.00. One day licenses will be available for $5.00.
