Hot Laps Mountain Bike Jam

Saturday, July 29th 2006

Snowbowl Ski & Summer Resort

Missoula, Montana

Hot Laps Mountain Bike Jam is an official NORBA event and

Will be conducted under NORBA competition rules.

Call 543-3331 for more information—www.snowbowl.com

Circuit Race featuring Snowbowl’s Single Track Trail

Course will be up single track trail “Beargrass Highway” and

Down “2nd Thought”

9:30 Registration: at the “Last Run”

11:30 am Mass Start: all classes start together at the base of

Snowbowl. Experts will do two laps beginners will take an easier way down.  Proper downhill personal protective equipment is

required, i.e. helmet, eye wear, etc.  Call Big Sky Cyclery if unsure. 

4pm Awards party:  $500 guaranteed cash purse for expert class plus lots of prizes for sport and beginners. 

 

Entry fee:  $25 (includes T-shirt, BBQ, NORBA insurance, and live band)

$5 Late Fee after 7/27/06.
