The Bohart Bash -- Charity Mountain Bike Race

Sunday, July 13

Bohart Ranch

 

It's back and better than ever. The Bohart Bash is a charity mountain bike

race that's organized by Team WinS. Proceeds go to AWARE's youth recreation

fund. The 8 km course is a combination of single-track and double-track

suitable for all skill levels. We've had women come and do the race after

taking the mountain bike skills clinic. Our first timer category lets you

try out the race by only doing one lap! Register by filling out a form

(available at most bike shops around town) and returning it by mail or

register in-person on Saturday, July 12 from 5 to 7 p.m. at the Ridge

Downtown on Mendenhall or register on the day of the race between 8:30 and

9:30 a.m. In addition to a fun race, the Bohart Bash is also known for its

most excellent post-race raffle. Finally we have a great kids race (ages 5

to 11) so consider signing yourself and your child up to race!

 

Not interested in racing, but would still like to be part of the Bohart

Bash? Consider volunteering. We can use help with everything from

registering people the night before the race to cutting up fruit for the

racers to eat! For more information, contact jennymccune@imt.net or give her

a call at 586-7044.
