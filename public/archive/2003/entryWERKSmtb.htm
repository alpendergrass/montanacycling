WERKS Cross-Country Mountain Bike Race

Saturday June 21, 2003

Great Divide Ski Area, Just outside of Helena, Montana 

 

NEW FOR 2003:  

·        A post race BB-Q on the deck !  included with registration. 

·        Take advantage of the Helena Music Festival, We've changed the race from Sunday to Saturday so that racers can attend the music festival Saturday night guilt free  Check it  out:

   www.downtownhelena.com/musicfest2003.htm

·        New Course:  Five mile fun loop that features great views and beautiful flowing single track.

 

Directions:    From I-15 north of Helena, take exit 200, Lincoln Road, (Highway 279) then west about 10.5 miles to Marysville Road. Watch for Ski Area  colorful sign on the left (south) side of the highway about 1/2 a mile west of the intersection with Birdseye Road. Turn on Marysville Road and drive 7 miles to Great Divide.Those in Helena may also reach Lincoln Road by using Montana Avenue, Green Meadow Drive, or Birdseye Road.

 

General Information:

Race Day Registration:   9:30 - 10:30 at Great Divide Ski Area

Entry Fee:   $25

Race Start:  11:00  staggered start every 5 minutes

  • 1 lap Beginner
  • 3 laps Sport
  • 5 laps Expert/Elite

Prizes:    $1000 in cash and prizes

Cash Prize for Expert/Elite
