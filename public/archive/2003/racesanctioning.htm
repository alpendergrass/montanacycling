A couple people have contacted me wondering what the deal is now with USCF permit applications.

 

Well, here's the deal:

 

1.    Under the terms of MBRA's agreement with the USCF, MBRA needs a USCF permitting coordinator. So now we have one. I am he.

2.    The idea is not to create another layer of bureaucracy, the idea is to help MBRA members put on better and safer events.

3.       Filling out a permit application is not difficult. The only variable is figuring out the application fee. The event website can always be www.montanacycling.org

4.      Promoters should fill out, sign, and send the permit application, just like they have in the past. Just let me know you are doing it.

5.    Any promoter who has a question about the permit application process is welcome to ask me for help.

6.    As permitting coordinator, I am basically in charge of MBRA's race schedule. MBRA teams will schedule events that do not conflict with other MBRA events, and do not conflict with non-MBRA events at which a bunch of MBRA riders generally tend to want to race. Basically, we have that schedule worked out now. Promoters generally have dibs on certain weekends based on past years, and there is no real reason to change that unless promoters want to change dates, and we can work that out among us.

7.    So if a MBRA team wants to put on a new event or change dates, just bounce the idea on the listserv and we can work out dates.

8.    I would like promoters of races that have been held in past years to send me a race schedule and safety plan (plans for medical assistance, course marshalls, cops, road issues, etc.) when they send in their USCF permit application. I'll review the schedule and safety plan and make suggestions if I have any.

9.    I would like promoters of a new race to send me the schedule and safety plan before they apply for a USCF permit, so I can make sure that these have been thought out prior to applying for a permit.

 

That's all. If people want me to have a different role, I am open to

suggestions.

 

And if you are aware of racers and race team members who are not on this listserv, tell them to sign on.
