MBRA Cross #9 is this Sunday in Helena.

 

The season finale, MBRA Cross #10, will be Sunday November 23 at McCormick Park in Missoula.

Registration starts at 11, racing starts at noon. $15 entry fee.

 

The course will be wily and winding willy-nilly up and down around the park. The course will feature precipitous drops, dangerous turns, forbidden singletrack, carpet-smooth turf, collarbone-shattering tree roots, broken glass, and boiling lava. Jamul, Clint, and Geoff have lapped me in every race I've been in this year, so I'm determined to set a course where I can ride just as slow as usual and not get lapped. So expect an inordinate amount of dismounts, sandpits, rockpiles, river crossings, run-ups and other spectator-friendly attractions.

 

MBRA 2003 Cross Series awards will be presented immediately following at post-race festivities.

It looks like the Mens A series winner will skim home with at least $100. With two races to go, there are three riders still in the hunt for the series win. It could all boil down to the last lap in Missoula.

 

The drawing for the Bianchi cross bike will also take place at post-race festivities. All riders get one automatic entry in the raffle for every race entered, and all volunteers get one automatic entry for every race they helped on. Tickets are also available for $2 or 6 for $10.
