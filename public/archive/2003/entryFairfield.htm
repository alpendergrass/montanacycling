Fifth Annual

Fairfield Road Race

A Bicycle Race for all Cyclists

 Saturday, May 10, 2003

USCF Permit Pending

$300 - $1,000  Cash Purse

 

RACE DESCRIPTION:   The race course follows remote secondary roads through rural areas southwest of the town of Fairfield, MT in the shadow of the Rocky Mountain Front.  The course  follows scenic,  smooth, lightly traveled highways through rolling terrain with a few climbs near the halfway point.  Distances:  Category 2,3 - 78 miles.      Other Categories - 52 miles

 

DIRECTIONS:  Stage at City Park on the East side of Fairfield.  The park is located on the east end of Main Street.   Registration opens at 10:00 am.  Racing will start at 11:00 am.  Awards after the race at O’Henry’s Bar & Cafe on Main Street.

 

MISCELLANEOUS:  Montana Cup Points Series Event.  USA Cycling rules apply.  One Day and Annual licenses will be available.  Total registration fee $20.00.   Race day registration only.  All ages and abilities are eligible to enter.  Promoted by the Echelon Cycling Club.  Call Rich West, (406) 467-3673 or e-mail johnjuras@aol.com if you need more information.

 

INCREASING CASH PURSE: If 40 to 49 USCF racers register, the cash purse will increase to $400.  Similar increases for increments of 10 riders thereafter.  We expect over 60 riders this year.
