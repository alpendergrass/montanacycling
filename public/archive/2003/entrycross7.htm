MBRA CYCLOCROSS RACE #7

 

 

Sunday, November 2, 2003

12:00 p.m.

DNRC Campus

Missoula

 

 

 

This criterium-like course is flat and fast, with lap lengths of 3k (approx. 6 minutes). It features a nice mix of surfaces (60% dirt/gravel, 25% asphalt, 15% grass), and only a few, minor technical challenges. It’s a great opportunity for beginners to try out the sport, and for experienced ‘crossers to race with an intact pack. Mountain bikes are welcome – just be sure to remove your bar-ends. The venue is spectator-friendly, too, with numerous locations to view the action up close. So, come on out and join the fun!

 

 

·        Race Categories: Men A, Men B, Women, Masters (Men 40+)

 

·        Start Time: 12:00 p.m. (separate category starts if racer numbers warrant)

 

·        USCF or NORBA licenses required (one-day licenses available for $5)

 

·        Registration: Day of race, on site, 10:30-11:45 a.m.

 

·        Entry Fee:  $15 (includes USCF insurance surcharge)

 

·        Prizes for top finishers!!!  Payout dependent upon turnout

 

 

Location: Dept. of Natural Resources & Conservation (DNRC) campus, in Missoula

      Spurgin Road at 27th Avenue – 2 blocks west of Reserve, 6 blocks north of South Ave.                  Entrance/parking is on 27th Ave.
