Dear RATPOD veterans:

 

     Thanks to your overwhelming support and positive feedback, RATPOD 2003 will roll through the Big Hole valley once again on June 28, 2003.  The website is now functioning at full capacity thanks to the generous support of Garrett Hart in Indianapolis, Indiana.   You can now register online www.ratpod.org   Please visit the website and view pictures and a press release  from last year as well as a new "map" section. A water stop has been added this year in Fishtrap.  Our brochures and posters are going to press today and will be distributed to local bike shops and fitness centers. The pasta feed will again be held at the Elk's Lodge and the post ride BBQ will feature Tom Catmull and Larry Hirschberg. The BBQ will begin at 6:00 PM and carry through until about 10:00 PM. The entry fee for RATPOD 2003 will be $70 and will include the pasta feed and the BBQ.  Gold level sponsors for RATPOD 2003 include Missoula Bone and Joint as well as Missoula Anesthesiology.
