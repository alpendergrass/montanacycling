EVENT DESCRIPTION

The Helena Twilight Criterium course is located in the Capitol Complex in Helena, Montana. The 0.7-mile almost flat course is set near the Capitol among shady tree-lined roads of the Capitol Complex, and will provide a fast and fun course for all racers. The race will also serve as the District 26 Criterium Championships. Registration is located near the course at rear of the Capitol Building on Lockey Avenue. There will also be two free fun races for children under 10.
