Western Montana Spring Training Series - Missoula Montana

* Saturday March 18 - Turah Time Trial - 11 miles.

* Saturday March 24 - Butler Creek Circuit Race I - 1 Hour + 1 lap.

* Saturday April 8 - Clinton Road Race 43 Miles.

* Saturday April 15 - Butler Creek Circuit Race II - 1 Hour + 2 laps.

 

Start time for all races is 12:00 noon.

Registration for all races will be at the Bike Doctor 420 N. Higgins Ave. (Higgins & Pine) from 10:30 - 11:00.

 

Course Descriptions:

* Turah Time Trial - Flat 5.5 mile out and back on lightly traveled frontage road. Start is off Hwy. 210 at Turah frontage road.

* Butler Creek Circuit - Hilly 1.7 mile circuit. Start is at intersection of Expressway Ave. and Butler Creek Rd.

* Clinton Road Race - Mostly flat 8 mile circuit. Start is in Clinton at intersection of frontage road and I-90 overpass..

 

Entry fee: $5.00 per race.

Categories are:

* A - USCF 1 - 3.

* B - Women, USCF 4, 5, and Juniors.

Masters race their USCF category.

 

All categories start together and finish on the same lap.

Butler Creek races may be handicapped with category B riders given a head start.

Lapped riders are encouraged to continue, but no contesting final main field sprint.

Free lap for mechanical problems except for the Clinton RR.

Open to all cyclists, regardless of ability level. The purpose of this series is to gain experience and fitness with low key, fun races.

Cyclists are encouraged to ride to the races from registration and continue after with a training ride through the lovely Montana countryside.

 

Races are sponsored by Five Valley Velo
