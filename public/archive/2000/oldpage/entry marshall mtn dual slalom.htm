Five Valley Velo presents:

Marshall Mountain Snow Dual Slalom

Marshall Mountain Ski Area

March 11, 2000

Start Time for races begins at 10:00 a.m.

Registration will be held at 8:00 a.m. to 9:30 a.m. the day of the race in

"The Pub" at Marshall Mountain the day of the race.

Course Description:

Dual slalom course which will be held on snow and will include several jumps

and burmed turns. Vertical drop will be an estimated 500 feet.

Entry fee: $15.00 per racer

Catagories:

Beginner

Sport

Expert

Junior

Male and female racers will be divided into the appropiate catagory;

example:female sport vs. female sport.

This will be a double elimination so riders get at least two runs.
