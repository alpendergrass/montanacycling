MONTANA DISTRICT CHAMPIONSHIPS 

$300 CASH PRIZE LIST

DISTRICT TIME TRIAL CHAMPIONSHIP (June 17, 2000): Following the same course as last year’s District Time Trial, expect to "enjoy" the challenges dished out by 40K of the "Race of Truth." The course is smooth, mostly flat, slightly winding, and heads Southwesterly before returning Northeasterly. If the wind is blowing, you may want an ll tooth cog for the return trip. USCF Race Permit applied for.

DIRECTIONS: Stage at the Quigleys Quik Stop Exxon located at the Ulm Exit off Interstate 15, approximately 8 miles southwest of Great Falls.

REGISTRATION: Register outside the Exxon between 10:00 and 11:00 am. Please register early to avoid the rush. Start times posted at registration by 11:10. First rider starts at 11:20. Pre-registration is via BIKERIDE.COM only. Entry Fee is $15.00 or $25.00 for both the TT and RR.


DISTRICT ROAD RACE CHAMPIONSHIP (June 18, 2000): The Cow Country Classic course is once again available for our racing pleasure so get ready for one or two laps on this semi-mountainous race course. Category Pro, 1, 2, 3 complete two laps for a 98 mile total distance. All others complete one lap for 49 miles.

DIRECTIONS: Stage at the Holter Lake Road access parking area located 3 miles east of Wolf Creek on Recreation Road. Registration opens at 10:00 am. Groups start at 11:00, 11:10, and 11:20 from the staging area. Distances are 104 miles for Sr 2,3 and 57 miles for all other groups. Groups will be separated or combined on race day depending on field size. Entry fee is $15.00 or $25.00 for both the TT&RR.

COURSE DESCRIPTION: Racers proceed west from the staging area to Wolf Creek, then notrhwest on MT 434 to MT 200, turn right on MT to US 287, then right on US 287 to finish line atop 1 km uphill. Pro, 1, 2, 3 proceed onto I-15 to Wolf Creek for another lap.

RACE RULES: Centerline rule will be enforced, including at the finish. Neutral zone in Wolf Creek for all racers. All USA Cycling Rules apply. Racers MUST obey all traffic laws.

AWARDS: Cash prizes awarded to top racers in each category based upon total of MT Cup points earned in the TT and RR. Racers do not have to enter both events to be eligible for cash prizes. The "Lamier Rule" is also in effect (no points transferred between categories). District Championship Medals will be awarded to the top finishers each category for both the RR and the TT.

SPONSORS: Please support our sponsors including Bighorn Wilderness, Knicker Biker, Central Bike, Pasta Montana, and Great Harvest Bread!

OTHER: Promoted by the Echelon Cycling Club, Inc. Contact John Juras at johnjuras@aol.com or 761-7021 (before 9 pm only) if you have questions. Entry Fee is $15.00 for riding just the RR. This includes USCF insurance surcharge.
