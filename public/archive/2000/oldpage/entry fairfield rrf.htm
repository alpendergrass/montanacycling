Fairfield Road Race Saturday, May 13, 2000

Fairfield, MT

$400 Cash Purse

 

RACE DESCRIPTION: The out-and-back course follows remote secondary roads through rural areas west of the town of Fairfield, MT in the shadow of the Rocky Mountain Front. The course follows scenic, smooth, lightly traveled highways through rolling terrain with a few climbs near the halfway point.

Distances: Category 2,3 – 70 miles. Other Categories – 52 miles

DIRECTIONS: Stage at City Park on the East side of Fairfield. The park is located on the east end of Main Street. Registration opens at 10:00 am. The Cat 1, 2, 3’s start at 11:00 am followed by the remaining categories.

MISCELLANEOUS: Montana Cup Points Series Event. USA Cycling rules apply. Total registration fee $15.00. Pre-registration via racegate.com only. Race day registration welcome. All ages and abilities are eligible to enter. Promoted by the Echelon Cycling Club. Call Rich West, (406) 467-3673 or e-mail johnjuras@aol.com if you need more information.

SPONSORS: Please support our sponsors including Bighorn Wilderness, Knicker Biker, Central Bike, Pasta Montana, and Great Harvest Bread!
