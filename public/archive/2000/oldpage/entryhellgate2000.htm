The Hellgate Mountainbike Duathlon

WHAT ?

A challenging Run-Bike-Run. Starts with a 4k trail run followed by a 15k mountain bike and finishing with another 4k trail run. This race is sanctioned by USA Triathlon.

WHEN ?

Saturday, October 14th at 10:30 am

Registration and Packet Pick-Up begins at 8:30 am. Race day registration will be allowed if the race is not full.

WHERE ?

35 minutes west of Missoula, Montana at Kreis Pond Mountain Bike Trails in the Niinemile Ranger District of Lolo National Forest. Take exit 82 off of Interstate 90 then follow the signs.

Questions??? Call Jeff at 406.728.5790

Brought to you by:

Five Valley Velo / Team Stampede.

 

THE COURSE

The run is 100% rolling trails with a large down-hill and a large uphill. The bike course is a mix of winding fast single-track, fire road climbs, and fast downhills. It is not extremely technical, however, we encourage everyone to ride the course before the race.

OTHER STUFF

NO HELMET = NO RACE
