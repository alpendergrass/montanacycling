Directions to race area:

Exit Interstate 90 at Exit 452 and turn north onto Hwy 87 towards Billings. Follow Hwy 87 approximately 1.2 miles to intersection with 1st Ave North and Main Street (also Hwy 87 North. Metra Park & Fairgrounds will be to immediate right).

Turn right and follow Main Street (also marked as Hwy 87 North to Roundup). Follow Main Street through Billings Heights approximately 3.5 miles to intersection of Hwy 87 North and Hwy 312 East.

Follow Hwy 312 appr. 8 miles to intersection with Shepherd Road (marked with blinking light) and turn left (north).

Go north on Shepherd Road appr. 4 miles to Scandia Road and turn right (east).

Follow Scandia road appr 2 miles to intersection with CA Road and turn left (north) onto CA road.

Follow CA road for 2 miles and then it will turn to gravel and then follow gravel for approximately 1 mile. Parking area is on left side.
