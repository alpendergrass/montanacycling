A MONTANA CUP SERIES EVENT

"My legs feel better after a TREK BARR!"

  • PROMOTER Brink Kuchenbrod, (406) 543-2385, kuch@bigsky.net (No calls after 9:00 PM, please.)
  • USCF SANCTIONED EVENT Held under U.S. Cycling Federation race permit.
  • COURSE This year’s Montana Velo road event will be held on rolling secondary roads in the Frenchtown, MT area.
  • REGISTRATION Registration is at Frenchtown Ponds State Park from 9:00 AM to 10:30 AM at the WEST end of the park. To get there take the Frenchtown exit off I-90 (Frenchtown is 15 miles west of Missoula). Frenchtown Ponds State Park is on the north side of the interstate, 2 miles west of the exit. No pre-registration is necessary. The entry fee is $17 and includes the $2 insurance surcharge.
