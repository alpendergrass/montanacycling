RESULTS FROM MONTANA SUPER CUP CYCLOCROSS NUMBER ONE

 

The first race in the Montana Super Cup cyclocross series began on

Sunday with a good turnout of 23 riders. Mark "Dirty Kiwi" Legg

established his lead early on the first lap and never looked back,

lapping all but six riders by the finish. Chad Devall from Hamilton

outlasted Jake "Mega Watt" Hartsoch of Missoula for second with an

impressive surge at the halfway point. In the women's race, Margie Legg

used her off-road experience to best first time crossers Jennifer Carey

and Eryn Hanna.

Northern Rockies would not have been able to put on this race without

the help of Jeff Crouch, Randy Ristau, Jon Berman, Amber Lowery and most

importantly Carl "Big Daddy" Ammons. Thanks to all who came out!
