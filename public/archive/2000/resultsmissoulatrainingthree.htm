MISSOULA TRAINING RACE NUMBER THREE RESULTS

Following are the results of the Missoula Training Series # 1, Clinton

Road Race, April 9 - 43 miles - 35 entrants handicapped start, two train

crossings. My apologies for the limited results, but only the top four

places were recorded. Thanks to everyone for coming out, we had 3 times

as many entrants as the Spokane Roubaix held the day before. I hope you

all had fun.

Don't forget the last race of this series Saturday April 15.

Registration 10:30 - 11:00 at the Bike Doctor. Race 12:00 at the Butler

Creek loop, finish at the ski station.

Special thanks to our volunteers Mark Legg and Steve Dolberg for their

time and effort. Also to Big Dipper Ice Cream for prizes.
