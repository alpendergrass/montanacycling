Marcus Daly Criterium

 

The Marcus Daly Crieterium was held on Sunday July 23rd. The course consisted of a 6 turn L shaped course

located in the middle of downtown Hamilton. The Mens A race was won by Geoff Proctor who had won the Marcus Daly Time

Trial the evening before, The Mens B race was won by Steve Rapp who came from Walla Walla, Washington, and the women's

race was won by Kathy McGann of Missoula.
