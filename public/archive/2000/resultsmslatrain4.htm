Attached are the results of the Missoula Training Series #4, Butler

Creek Circuit Race April 15. Handicapped start, one hour plus two laps,

hilltop finish.

Thanks to everyone for coming out for this series and making it a

success (I think). If anyone has any suggestions, comments, or

criticisms to help make this a better series next year, please don't

hesitate to contact me.

Special thanks to our volunteers Erin McGlinchey and Jed Dennison and

their assistants Amber, Jake, and Keifer for their time and effort.

--

Fred
