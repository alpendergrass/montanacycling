The Northern Rockies Orthopedics Racing Team

 

presents the

 

Montana State Championship Criterium

 

Sunday, August 21, 2005

 

 

Race sanctioned by USA Cycling                                                 USCF permit pending

 

The Course: 

 

The race will take place at the Fort Missoula grounds, on the roads around the museum

 

Race Schedule:

Masters - 8:00am - 30min + 1 lap

Women 1,2,3,4 - 8:45 - 30min + 1 lap

Men 4,5 & Juniors - 9:30 - 30min + 1 lap

Men 1,2,3 - 10:15 - 50min + 1 lap

Registration:

 

Opens at 7:00 a.m. and closes 30 minutes before each respective race!!!

 

Fees: 

 

$15 race entry, includes USCF insurance surcharge.  USCF annual or one-day licenses required and available for purchase on-site.  MBRA memberships and permanent numbers also available at race.  Racers without permanent MBRA numbers must purchase a one-day number for $2.

 

Prizes:  t.b.d…

 

Other Details:

 

All USCF rules apply.  Free lap rule in effect until five (5) laps to go.

Racers must wear helmets while racing or warming up/down.

Race is part of the Subaru Montana Cup Series – results are worth double points!

Racers must hold a USCF Annual license to be eligible for championship medals.

 

 

Important Note:

 

Immediately following the Criterium, MBRA will host a BBQ picnic where we’ll crown the winners of the Subaru Montana Cup Series, hand out over $2000 in random drawing prizes (winners need not be present, but are responsible for obtaining prizes), and hold our annual meeting.

 

For more information:

Contact Mike Longmire (406) 544-0221

 

Directions:

 

In Missoula, take Hwy. 93 (Reserve Street) to South Avenue.  Head west, past Community Medical Center and soccer/rugby field to diagonal paved drive on south (left) side of road.  Follow this drive to the Ft. Missoula Museum and race parking.
