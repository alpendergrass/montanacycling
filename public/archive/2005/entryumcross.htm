University of Montana Cycling Club Presents

2005 Grizzly Cyclocross Race

Sunday, October 23rd

 
 

Mountain Bikes welcome but please remove bar-ends.

 

$20 Entry Fee ($5 for Juniors)

Checks Payable to UM Cycling

 

One-Day Licenses available for $5

 

Prizes to top-3 in each category

 

All entrants entered into raffle for most excellent prizes and swag

 

Men A will start at 11am, all others will start 2 minutes after

 

Race length is 1-hour + 1 lap

 

All prizes must be picked up at awards ceremony that will follow shortly after the conclusion of the race

 

MBRA Members please use MBRA numbers.

 

 
 

Race will be held at the Missoula Department of Natural Resources and Conservation.  Registration is from 9:45AM – 10:30AM; the race will begin at 11:00AM sharp.  Course is a good mix of wide opens flats and a few tighter technical sections, but overall this should prove to be a fast race.

 
 
 

 

 

 

 

 

 


Directions to Race

From I-90, take Hwy 60 South on Reserve Street.  Turn right onto Spurgin Street and then Left on 27th Street.  The Department of Natural Resources and Conservation will be on your right.
