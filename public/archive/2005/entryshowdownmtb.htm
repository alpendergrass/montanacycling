Attention All Montana Bike Clubs and Shops!

 

 

 

The Great Falls Bicycle Club will host the "King of the Hill Mountain Bike Challenge at Showdown" on Saturday, July 23rd, 2005.

 

 

The cross-country mountain bike race will follow a 6-mile loop at Showdown Montana Ski Area, located on US Highway 89 at the top of King's Hill Pass in the Lewis and Clark National Forest.

 

 

 

Children's, Beginner, Sport and Expert Classes will be offered - maybe a hill-climb as well.  Registration fee TBD.

 

 

 

All proceeds to benefit trail building and maintenance in the Great Falls/Highwood/Little Belt region.

 

 

 

Food, Beverage, Music and Good Times to follow the race.

 

 

 

Keep your eyes open for more information, or contact Chris Leatham, Event Chair at 406.236.5930 (h), 406.236.5522 (w) or skineihart@hotmail.com.

 

 

 

 

 

Updates will also be posted on www.greatfallsbicycleclub.org.
