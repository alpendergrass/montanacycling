Bike racing continues tomorrow with the Placer Motors Primavera Race #2 on the Birdseye-Lincoln Rd-Green Meadow loop in Helena. Racers with the best point total over the two-day omnium will take home cash prizes. Registration is from 9-10:30 at Spring Meadow Lake and racing starts at 11 am. Host club Montana Velo applauds the patience of Helena-area motorists in support of Montana cycling.
