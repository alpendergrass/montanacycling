Clinton Training Races
March 19th and 26th, 2005
 
Registration:  Day of race, at Missoula Bicycle Works on 708 S Higgins, 10-11am
Races begin at 12:00pm (Noon)
Race Fee: $10 per race; includes USCF insurance surcharge.  
USCF annual or one-day licenses required and available for purchase on-site
 
March 19th 4 laps
March 26th 5 laps
(One lap = approx. 8 miles)
 
Categories:  A,B,C (and Women on the 26th)*

 

*promoter reserves the right to combine or expand race groupings as conditions, attendance and volunteer support warrant.

 

 

 

 

All USCF rules apply.  Racers must wear helmets while racing or warming up/down.  Junior gear restrictions will be enforced.

Racers with MBRA numbers are urged to wear them.  Place numbers on right side.
 
 

Directions:  Race staging area located approx. 20 miles east of Missoula on I-90.  Take I-90 to the Clinton exit, head south and turn right (west) onto old frontage road before crossing the railroad tracks.

 
Race proceeds go to the Montana Junior Cycling Foundation
