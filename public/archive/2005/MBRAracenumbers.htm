Montana Bicycle Racing Association

 

Race Numbering Protocol

 

 

Permanent Numbers:

 

 

MBRA Division                               MBRA Number Series

 

Men Cat. 1,2                                                                            1-30

 

Women Cat. 1,2,3,4                                                            101-125*

 

Men Masters A                                                                  201-230

 

Men Masters B                                                                  231-250

 

Men Cat. 3                                                                           301-325

 

Men Cat. 4,5                                                                        401-450**

 

 

* Cat. 4 women will be assigned numbers from the high end of this series  (i.e. 125, 124, 123, etc.)

 

** Juniors who are Cat. 4,5 will be assigned numbers from the high end of this series  (i.e. 450, 449, 448, etc.)

 

To assign a new, permanent MBRA race number:

 

1.        have racer complete top portion of MBRA membership application

2.        collect membership application and fee of $15 (cash, or check made out to MBRA)

3.        determine racers desired MBRA race division

4.        assign permanent MBRA race number, according to above schedule

5.        complete bottom portion of membership application with info from #3 and 4, above

6.        distribute set of lycra MBRA race numbers to racer (racer gets a pair of identical numbers)

7.        return completed application(s) and fee(s) to MBRA

 

 

 

 

 

 

Rental Numbers:

 

 

MBRA Divisions                                               Rental Number Series

 

Men Cat. 1,2 and Women                                                 560-599

 

Men Cat. 3 and Masters                                                   780-799

 

Men Cat. 4,5                                                                        450-499

 

 

To assign a one-day, rental race number:

 

  1. determine racers desired MBRA race division
  2. collect $2 number rental fee
  3. assign rental number, according to above schedule
  4. distribute one paper rental number to racer
  5. return fee(s) to MBRA
