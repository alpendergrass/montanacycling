Montana Cycling Email

List Server

If you want to receive and send messages regarding bike racing in Montana then join the Montana Cycling List Server. This enables you to receive and send emails to everyone who has signed up for the list.

The email address for the list server is montanacycling@yahoogroups.com.

          This service is free.

          To sign up send a blank email to montanacycling-subscribe@yahoogroups.com

          Or go to www.yahoogroups.com
