WEDNESDAY NIGHT MISSOULA SERIES INFO

 

Once again, the Missoula Cyclocross Series will kick the season off in style.

Wednesdays, 5:45 pm start time.

First race September 28

Five weeks of racing.

Last race just slips in before the return to Standard time.

Races will be 50 minutes plus a lap.

 

Series entry $20; individual race $5.

One-day licenses are $10, but you can get an annual good until Dec 31 for $30 if you don’t have a 2005 license.

 

Prizes: beer, cookies, bagels, doughnuts, bread, ice cream, trinkets, gizmos, and things of that nature.

Series prizes will be even bigger and better.

 

I am negotiating a new venue in the Rattlesnake valley, but if it falls through, all races will be at Ft Missoula. I’ll keep you posted.

 

There won’t be any Jamul to kick around this year, probably, nor any Tejay. Maybe we’ll see the 29-inch trio of Robertson, Beltz, and Hartman single-speed their Ramblum rovers around the course. Nah, I bet they’ll all ride cross bikes. So start your bunnyhopping and flying dismounts and Roy Rogers remounts and get ready for some sweet, strictly time-delimited racing.
