Host Club: Montana Velo

 

You want race opportunities? We got race opportunities.

 

Saturday Staging/Start: Rossiter School in Helena valley. (Same staging area as MTV RR 2002 & 2003). Directions: From Helena, go north on Montana Ave to right on Sierra Drive. Rossiter School is just before I-15 overpass. From Great Falls, take Lincoln Road exit and right on Lincoln Road to left on Montana. Go south until left on Sierra Drive. Registration will be in school parking lot.

 

Saturday Race Course: 20 mile flat loop out in Helena valley. One 2-3 mile section of firm gravel road and one 2 mile section of patched potholed road. Otherwise, very straightforward with only one very short climb. Normal gearing is fine (note: remember junior gearing restrictions). This course is open to vehicular traffic and racers have use of the right lane only. Yellow line violations will result in disqualification.

 

Sunday Staging/Start: Spring Meadow Lake Park in Helena, on west edge of town. (Same staging area as Helena Triathlon) Directions: From the west or east, follow Highway 12 and get yourself to the west end of town.  At farthest west traffic light, turn and go north on Joslyn St. Follow as it bends left and turns to Country Club Lane. Spring Meadow Lake Park is just a half-mile down on your left. Registration, bathrooms, and an opportunity to jump in the lake after the race are all here. Please don’t park on access street. Plenty of parking is available inside the park.

 

Sunday Race Course: 27 mile paved loop. Normal gearing is fine (note: remember junior gearing restrictions). One steady, mile-long, shallow grind on Birdseye Road and a few rollers. New finish near Birdseye/Lincoln Road intersection. Ride back to start parking approx. 10 miles/or plant extra car at Silver City Bar. 3 train crossings, two of which could see some action and one traffic light. Be aware of train and car traffic. This course is open to vehicular traffic and racers have use of the right lane only. Yellow line violations will result in disqualification.

 

Miscellaneous: Entry fee is $17 each day. Each race is a stand alone event for the Montana Subaru Cup; however, omnium scoring will be used for prize money. Points will be awarded for placings in each RR with highest cumulative points determining prize money in each division (3 deep). Sunday’s road race will carry slightly heavier points and will serve as a tie-break if necessary. Racers do not have to compete in both events to be eligible for prize money, though competing in both certainly improves one’s chances of finishing “in the money” overall. Prize money ceremony: after Sunday’s race at Spring Meadow Lake.

All USAC rules apply. One-day and annual licenses available at registration. Helmets at all times or DQ.

 

Lodging:  Holiday Inn Helena Downtown—406.443.2200. $72 single; $78 double; $6 each additional person; kids eat all meals free. For reservations, use code MTV when you call.
